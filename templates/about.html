We want to answer the question every student has before enrolling in a class: "How bad is it going to be?" Our app gives an estimate of how difficult a particular course at UCI might be according to past students.

Using the RateMyProfessor and PeterPortal APIs, we can give a rough estimate of the average difficulty of a class based on the professors who have taught it before.

The difficulty scores are based on the reviews of past students who have taken the same course at UCI.
