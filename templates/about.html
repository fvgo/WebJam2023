ABOUT

Our mission is to help students at the UCI make informed decisions during course registration by providing a valuable and accessible tool for estimating course difficulty.

AnteaterSanityCheck is committed to enhancing the student experience at UCI by equipping students with the knowledge they need to thrive academically, creating a community where informed decision-making leads to academic success.

Using the RateMyProfessor and PeterPortal APIs, we are able to provide a rough estimate of the average difficulty of a course by consolidating student reviews on the professors who have taught it before.

Simply navigate to the "Courses" page of our website, input the department and class number of the course you want to check, and we'll give you the course's overall difficulty rating!

We've got the answer to the question on every student's mind before enrolling in a course: "How bad is it going to be?"

And who would provide better insight than students who have taken it before?

Our app offers an estimate of how difficult a particular course at UCI might based on feedback from past students, so Anteaters can make more informed decisions during registration, ensuring a smoother academic journey.

The heart of AnteaterSanityCheck lies in the experiences of past students.

Our difficulty scores are retrieved from comprehensive reviews, providing you with a detailed breakdown of each professor who has taught the course and their individual difficulty ratings.

This allows you to compare and contrast teaching styles, helping you identify which professors might present a more challenging learning experience.
